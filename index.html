210697 Installation Guide

Overview

Duct Installation Kit (Vaisala item 210697) is designed to support the probe head of a cabled probe so it can be installed to a chamber or duct. The kit is compatible with Vaisala probes that have a M9×1 body thread at the rear of the probe head. For example, the following products are compatible: HMT333 and HMT337 humidity and temperature transmitter HMP3 and HMP7 humidity and temperature probe

Probe Mounting Using the Duct Installation Kit

Thread the clamping bush, nut, and supporting bar onto the probe cable.

Duct Installation Kit 210697 Dimensions

HMT333 and HMT337 humidity and temperature transmitter HMP3 and HMP7 humidity and temperature probe

Step 1

Thread the clamping bush, nut, and supporting bar onto the probe cable.

  1. M9×1 thread on probe head
  2. Supporting bar
  3. Clamping bush
  4. Nut

Step 2

Thread the hanger to the probe and then thread the supporting bar to the hanger. Fasten the bushing around the cable and the supporting bar.

  1. Hanger
  2. Bushing

Step 3

Pass the probe through the flange, and thread the nut to the flange.

  1. Flange
  2. Clamping bush
  3. Bushing
  4. Nut

Step 4

Fasten the seal to the flange.

  1. Seal
  2. Flange
  3. Plastic washer, 4 pcs
  4. Washer, 4 pcs
  5. Screw, 4 pcs

Step 5

Make a Ø 20 mm hole in the surface of the chamber or duct.

Step 6

Insert the probe through the hole and fasten the flange using screws and washers.
